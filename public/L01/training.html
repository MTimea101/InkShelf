Training Tips

Learn how to train your dog with positive reinforcement techniques.

🐾 Basic Commands

  • Sit – Teach your dog to sit by using treats and a hand signal.
  • Stay – Help your dog learn self-control and patience.
  • Come – A crucial command for recall and safety.
  • Heel – Train your dog to walk calmly on a leash.

🐾 Positive Reinforcement

Reward-based training is the most effective method to encourage good behavior in dogs. Use treats, praise, and toys to reinforce commands.

🐾 Socialization

Introduce your dog to new environments, people, and other dogs early to ensure a well-adjusted and confident pet.
